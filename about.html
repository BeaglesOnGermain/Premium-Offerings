About Sandra Melvin, CLC

Compassion, clarity, and deep connection.

A gentle intuitive guide rooted in Saint John, serving clients locally and worldwide.

Meet Sandra

A grounded presence for tender turning points.

For over 20 years, Sandra has supported people and their animal companions through intuitive guidance, energy work, compassionate listening, and spiritual connection.

Rooted in a lifelong connection to spirit, the presence of God, nature, and animals, Sandra brings warmth, steadiness, and emotional intelligence to the work.

People often come feeling stuck, overwhelmed, disconnected, or unsure. The intention is that they leave feeling clearer, more grounded, and gently reminded of their own worth and inner knowing.
