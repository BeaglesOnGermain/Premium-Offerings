Coffee & Clarity was born from a simple belief: when people feel safe, unhurried, and genuinely heard, clarity begins to surface.

This is not a rushed reading or a performance. It is a private, compassionate experience for the tender places in life — decisions, transitions, grief, uncertainty, relationships, animal companions, and the quiet questions you may not know how to say out loud yet.

Based in Saint John and available worldwide, each session is held with the grounded warmth of a Maritime conversation and the care of a sacred appointment.
