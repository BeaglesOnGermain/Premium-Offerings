The Coffee & Clarity Method

A private experience held before, during, and after.

Your session is not just the time on the calendar. It is a thoughtfully held process designed to help you prepare, receive, and integrate.

01

Prepare

You receive a personalized pre-session meditation or prayer created around your intention, question, or concern.

02

Hold

Before we meet, I spend dedicated time in meditation or prayer on your behalf, holding your concern with compassion and openness.

03

Receive

Our live session blends intuitive guidance, deep listening, reflection, spiritual insight, and practical encouragement.

04

Integrate

Afterward, you receive a Clarity Action Plan with key themes, gentle next steps, and supportive practices based on our conversation.

Premium does not have to mean cold.

For Coffee & Clarity, premium means spaciousness, privacy, preparation, presence, and follow-through. It means you are not squeezed into a quick appointment and sent away to make sense of everything alone.

Be held with care from beginning to end.

This is a gentle, structured, deeply personal way to receive intuitive support.
